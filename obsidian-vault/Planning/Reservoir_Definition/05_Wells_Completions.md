# Wells and Completions - Eagle West Field

## Executive Summary

The Eagle West Field development strategy incorporates a balanced producer-injector configuration designed to optimize recovery from the three-sand reservoir system. The well completion design emphasizes selective perforation strategies to maximize contact with productive zones while maintaining operational flexibility for enhanced recovery operations.

## 2. Completion Design

### 2.1 PROD1 Completion Specifications

#### Perforation Program
- **Perforated Interval**: 7,950 - 8,115 ft TVD (165 ft net)
- **Zones Perforated**:
  - Upper Sand: 7,950 - 8,015 ft (65 ft)
  - Middle Sand: 8,025 - 8,070 ft (45 ft)
  - Lower Sand: 8,080 - 8,115 ft (35 ft)
- **Perforation Type**: Deep penetrating shaped charges
- **Shot Density**: 4 shots per foot (SPF)
- **Phasing**: 60° spiral phasing
- **Hole Size**: 0.43" entrance, 0.65" diameter at 12" penetration

#### Casing Program
- **Surface Casing**: 13⅜" at 2,500 ft, cemented to surface
- **Intermediate Casing**: 9⅝" at 7,800 ft, cemented to surface
- **Production Casing**: 7" at 8,200 ft TD, cemented across pay zones
- **Tubing**: 4½" N-80 EUE premium connections
- **Packer**: Retrievable production packer at 7,900 ft

### 2.2 INJ1 Completion Specifications

  - Aquifer Contact: 8,115 - 8,133 ft (18 ft)
- **Perforation Type**: Big hole charges for injectivity
- **Shot Density**: 6 shots per foot (SPF)
- **Phasing**: 45° spiral phasing

#### Casing Program

- **Intermediate Casing**: 9⅝" at 7,800 ft, cemented to surface
- **Injection Casing**: 7" at 8,300 ft TD, cemented to surface
- **Tubing**: 5½" P-110 premium connections for high pressure service
- **Packer**: Permanent injection packer at 7,950 ft

### 6.1 Well Indices Calculation

#### Peaceman Well Model Parameters
- **Well Index Formula**: WI = 2π × kh × T / (μ × ln(re/rw) + S)
- **Effective Wellbore Radius**: 0.35 ft (8.4" open hole equivalent)
- **Drainage Radius**: 745 ft (calculated from well spacing)
- **Grid Block Size**: 200 ft × 200 ft × variable thickness
